Why develop for mobile?

  • Mobile is everywhere
  • People are addicted to their mobile devices
  • You can do awesome things

Hey, I'm convinced! Where do I sign up?

  • Not so fast. It's not all unicorns and lollipops.

You thought IE6 was bad?

Most mobile browsers are terrible

  • Some of the worst browsers still in use are on mobile devices
  • Most Windows phones ship with IE5
  • Windows Phone 7 uses IE7
  • CSS/JS on many Blackberries disabled by default
  • Openwave? Symbian? That's it, I quit.

(Almost) Everything You Know is Wrong

  • Web standards support is laughable on many devices
  • Tables for layout? WTF!?
  • Sometime, user-agent sniffing is all you have
  • Progressive Enhancement will only get you so far, and it's not far at all

It's not all bad

  • Some of the best browsers in the world are on mobile devices
  • Imagine a world without legacy browsers
  • Webkit is everywhere on mobile*
  • Mozilla is... not, but they could be
* At least in the West

Travel at the speed of the future

Design Considerations for Mobile Sites

  • Remember 56kpbs modems?
  • A responsive UI is critical
  • Pickuppability (and putdownability)
  • Always be: Clear, Concise, & (c)Useful
CAN I USE YOUR WEBZONE!?!?!

Know your audience

  • Who are your users?
  • Are there people you want to use you but can't?
  • Just under half of all mobile web traffic is from smartphones
