Why develop for mobile?

  • Mobile is everywhere
  • People are addicted to their mobile devices
  • You can do awesome things

(Almost) Everything You Know is Wrong

  • Tables for layout? WTF!?
  • User-agent sniffing is all you have
  • Progressive Enhancement will only get you so far

You thought IE6 was bad?

The worst browsers are on mobile devices

  • Most Windows phones ship with IE5
  • Windows Phone 7 uses IE7
  • No CSS/JS on most Blackberries
  • Openwave? Symbian? That's it, I quit.

It's not all bad

  • Imagine a world without legacy browsers
  • Webkit is everywhere on mobile*
  • Mozilla is... not, but they could be
* At least in the West

Design Considerations for Mobile Sites

  • Remember 56kpbs modems?
  • A responsive UI is critical
  • Pickuppability (and putdownability)
  • Always be: Clear, Concise, & (c)Useful

Know your audience

  • Who are your users?
  • Are there people you want to use you but can't?
